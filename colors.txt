# Colors used in GitHub to Dockerfile Generator Frontend - Blue Theme

## Primary Brand Colors
#F8FAFF - Main background (light blue/off-white)
#FE4A60 - Brand red accent (for "2" in title)
#4A90E2 - Brand blue accent (for "Dockerfile" in title)

## Neutral Colors
#FFFFFF - Pure white (Monaco editor background, streaming content)
#F0F4FF - Light blue-gray background (results containers)
#E6F2FF - Light blue (form backgrounds, info cards)
#5A9BF5 - Primary blue (buttons, loader accent)

## Gray Scale
#000000 - Pure black (loading shadows)
#1F2937 - Dark gray (Monaco editor borders)
#374151 - Gray-700 (equivalent to gray-900 in some contexts)
#4B5563 - Gray-600 (text, placeholders)
#6B7280 - Gray-500 (borders)
#9CA3AF - Gray-400 
#D1D5DB - Gray-300 (inactive borders)
#E5E7EB - Gray-200
#F3F4F6 - Gray-100
#F9FAFB - Gray-50

## Status Colors
#FEF2F2 - Red-50 (error backgrounds)
#DC2626 - Red-600 (error text)
#EF4444 - Red-500 (error borders)
#B91C1C - Red-700 (error text)

#F0FDF4 - Green-50 (success backgrounds)
#10B981 - Green-500 (success borders)

#FFF4E6 - Orange-50 (active/warning backgrounds)
#F59E0B - Orange-500 (active/warning borders)

## Loader Animation Colors
#E6F2FF - Loader border base (light blue)
#5A9BF5 - Loader spinning accent (primary blue)
